Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology is an autonomous engineering college in Bangalore, Karnataka, India affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi.


Admission for all Indian students is as per the norms of the Government of Karnataka (GoK). NMIT admits students through Entrance tests such as CET, COMED-K, PGCET, GATE, KMAT and others. Students are also admitted under Management quota. NMIT (Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology) is a leading Engineering Institute that is ranked among the top 10 engineering colleges in Karnataka. NMIT is a private, self-financing and autonomous Institution that is approved by UGC (University Grants Commission) and AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education). NMIT programs are accredited by the NBA (National Board of Accreditation) and the Institute is accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC (National Assessment & Accreditation Council).
